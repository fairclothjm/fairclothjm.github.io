Workshop projects

Miscellaneous builds for my workshop.

2017

Saw horses

I built these saw horses with a handheld circular saw, a hammer, fasteners, and wood glue. Needless to say, for a simple project it was quite challenging. This was my first serious woodworking project.

These are extremely sturdy saw horses. I used the plans from Matthias Wandel.

Workbench

This workbench was also built with minimal tools. I mostly used a circular saw and electric drill. The workbench top is a repurposed tabletop from an old Walmart table that I was given years ago.

TTis is an extremely sturdy workbench. Again, I used the plans from Matthias Wandel.
